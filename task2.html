ALBERT EINSTEIN

INTRODUCTION:

BORN: March 14, 1879, Ulm, Germany

DIED:April 18, 1955 (age 76 years), Princeton, New Jersey, United States

EDUCATION:University of Zurich (1905), ETH Zürich (1897–1900)

AWARDS:Time 100: The Most Important People of the Century, Member of the National Academy of Sciences


CHILDHOOD AND EDUCATION:

Einstein’s parents were secular, middle-class Jews. His father, Hermann Einstein, was originally a featherbed salesman and later ran an electrochemical factory with moderate success. His mother, the former Pauline Koch, ran the family household. He had one sister, Maria (who went by the name Maja), born two years after Alber.
